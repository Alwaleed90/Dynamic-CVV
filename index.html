About the Idea

Traditional cards print static CVV codes. Once a fraudster sees or clones the card, they can use it endlessly online. Dynamic CVV eliminates this risk by generating temporary CVVs via the bank app or secure token, expiring after minutes or hours. No CVV is printed on the card.

How It Works

Step 1

User requests CVV through bank or app.

Step 2

App generates temporary CVV valid for limited time.

Step 3

Code disappears or regenerates (hourly/daily).

Step 4

No CVV printed on card â€“ reduces cloning & fraud.
